## 🌐 About Me

I'm a passionate tech builder from **Dhaka, Bangladesh 🇧🇩**, obsessed with creating immersive digital experiences that sit at the intersection of **engineering, design, and intelligence**.

### ✨ What Drives Me:

- 🧠 Curious about **AI, UX, and Data** — where they collide

- 🎨 **Design:** Figma, Adobe XD, UI/UX Principles
- 🤖 **Exploring:** AI/ML, LLMs, Web3
